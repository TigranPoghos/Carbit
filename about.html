Carbit — professional car service in Helsinki

Reliable maintenace and repair you can trust

Carbit is an auto service specializing in diagnostics and repair, with a strong focus on electrical systems, hybrid, and electric vehicles. The priority is safety, reliability, and consistent vehicle performance.
A wide range of services is covered — from brake system maintenance and component replacement to precise diagnostics and fault repair. Particular attention is given to electrical systems, as well as hybrid and EV service.
Each job is approached based on actual needs — no unnecessary work, with clear explanations at every stage.
Both routine maintenance and complex technical issues are handled with the same level of responsibility and attention to detail.Carbit is chosen by those who value transparency, technical accuracy, and predictable results.
