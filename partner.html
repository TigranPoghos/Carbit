About us

We are a professional auto repair service in Finland, specializing in vehicle maintenance and repair. We provide a full range of services — from routine maintenance to advanced diagnostics and complex repairs.
Our goal is to ensure the safety, reliability, and longevity of your vehicle. We work efficiently, transparently, and always offer the most effective solutions without unnecessary costs.
